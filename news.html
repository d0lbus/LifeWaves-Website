NUTEC Plastics progress on marine monitoring

NUTEC Plastics has expanded its monitoring from 18 to 139 beaches in Latin America and the Caribbean. Involving 63 countries, the initiative aims to improve global marine microplastics analysis by standardizing methods and sharing knowledge.

Study confirms sightings of endangered blue whale in Philippine waters

Recent research confirms that the blue whale nicknamed Bughaw, spotted in the Bohol Sea, is a pygmy blue whale. This finding suggests that the Bohol Sea may serve as a feeding ground for these whales. Further studies are needed to explore if it is also a breeding ground and to understand the species' migration patterns and conservation needs.

Pollution risks worsening global water scarcity

New research shows that water scarcity could affect up to three billion more people by mid-century due to nitrogen pollution, which worsens water quality. This pollution significantly increases the number of areas facing severe water scarcity. Improved fertilizer use, vegetarian diets, and better water treatment could help address this issue.
