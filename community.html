Opinions

"Marine ecosystems are the lungs of our planet, producing over half the oxygen we breathe. Protecting them is not only crucial for the health of our oceans, but for the survival of humanity itself. We must act now to curb pollution, overfishing, and the devastating impacts of climate change befor it's too late.

With every drop of water you drink, every breath you take, you're connected to the sea." - Dr. Sylvia Earle, renowed oceanographer and National Geographic Explorer-in-Residence

"The main global threats and impacts in marine environments are overfishing, climate change (global warming, ocean acidification), pollution (plastics), and habitat damage. These are also the main threats and impacts in marine environments that may inflict the greatest damage to society" - Sturla Kvamsdal, Arnt O. Hopland, Yuanhao Li, Simon Selle
